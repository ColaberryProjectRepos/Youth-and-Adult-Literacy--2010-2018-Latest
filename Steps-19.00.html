Visual 1. Line and Clustered Column (Gender)


Steps
  1. Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select Line and Clustered Column Chart
  2. Fill up figures provided below for the necessary fields
Build Visual Tab:
  • X-axis = Year
  • Column y-axis = Total Literacy%
  • Line y-axis =Literacy YoY%, M Literacy YoY, F Literacy YoY
  • Column legend= Gender-short (rename as Gender)
  • Tooltips = Literacy YoY%, M Literacy YoY, F Literacy YoY
  • After filling up the above rename fields, as per gif provided below
