Adding Title and Logos 14


To insert the Colaberry logo and the Literacy logo(globe) follow the following steps: 
1.From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select the 
Colaberry logo and click open.
2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.
Height=89-90
Width=193-239
Horizontal= 0
Vertical =0
Title=off
Effects->Background=on, Transparency=0%
Literacy Logo(globe) -
1.From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select the Literacy logo and click open.
2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.
Height=89-90
Width=145-921
Horizontal = 135-120
Vertical =0
Title=off
Effects->Background=on, Transparency=0%
