Key Insights -Negative 5



In 2016 Literacy Rate% in Latin America and the Caribbean Countries decreased by 1.88% compared to the previous year 2015. In 2015, Literacy Rate% increased by 0.03%. 




The senior age group (65+) has the lowest literacy rate(65%) compared to the other age groups. The Male literacy advantage is also higher (14.59%) than the other age groups in the years 2010-2018.
